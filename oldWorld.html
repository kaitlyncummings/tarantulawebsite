Old World Tarantulas

Old world tarantulas are named for where they are located in the wild. They come from the European, Asian, African, and Australian contients and their surrounding islands. These tarantulas generally pack a potent venom and are unwittingly fast. Old worlds are definitely not recommended for beginners, or of the faint of heart!

Cyriopagopus lividus

  • Common name: Cobalt Blue Tarantula
  • Origin: Southeast Asia, Borneo, the Philippines
  • Mature Diagonal Leg Span: 5 inches
  • Male Life Expenctancy: 5-6 years
  • Female Life Expectancy: 15-18 years
  • Enclosure Type: Terrestrial, obligatory burrower
  • Experience Level Needed: Advanced
