New World Tarantulas

New world tarantulas come from the North American and South American contients and their surrounding islands. These tarantulas generally have a vemon with low potentcy and urticating hairs for self defense. Tarantulas will flick urticating hairs from their abdomens as a form of self-defense. If these hairs make contact with your skin they can cause intense itching, rashes, and even allergic reactions. New world species generally tend to be better for beginners because they tend to be slower and less quick to strike!
